## Overall Homepage Citability Score: 18 / 100

> **Interpretation:** The Jonathan Y homepage is effectively citation-invisible to AI systems. It contains no answerable claims, no statistics, no factual guidance, and no quotable expertise. Every text block is either a marketing slogan, a product name, or a brief customer testimonial. AI systems (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Claude) will not cite this page in response to any user query — they simply have nothing to extract.

---

## Scoring Methodology

Each passage is scored on five dimensions (100 pts total):

| Dimension | Weight | What It Measures |
|-----------|--------|-----------------|
| Answer Block Quality | 25% | Does it directly answer a user question? |
| Self-Containment | 20% | Understandable without surrounding context? |
| Structural Readability | 20% | Can AI parse and extract cleanly? |
| Statistical Density | 20% | Specific numbers, measurements, verifiable facts? |
| Uniqueness / Originality | 15% | Original insight vs. generic category language? |

## What High-Citability Homepage Content Looks Like

For comparison, here is what the homepage *could* contain to earn a 65+ citability score:

### Improved (72/100):

### Why the improved version scores higher:
- Contains 4 specific facts (material, temperature range, cleaning time, size range)
- Answers: "Are Jonathan Y outdoor rugs durable?" (yes — why)
- Answers: "What sizes do Jonathan Y outdoor rugs come in?" (2×3 to 9×12)
- Self-contained: understandable without the rest of the page
- AI can quote the temperature range or cleaning claim directly
